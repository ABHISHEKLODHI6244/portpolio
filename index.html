About Me

Hello! My name is Abhishek Singh Lodhi, a passionate and dedicated software engineer with a B.Tech degree in Computer Science. I specialize in full-stack Java development and enjoy creating user-friendly, efficient digital solutions. Alongside my career in tech, I am also a traveler and a journalist, actively contributing to media with insightful and impactful stories. Driven by curiosity and purpose, I strive to grow through every experience—whether it's coding, exploring new destinations, or reporting stories that matter. My goal is to bring meaning, clarity, and integrity to everything I pursue.
